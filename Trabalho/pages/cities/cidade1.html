Vancouver

The harmonious coexistence of nature and urban life really draws attention to this city that cannot be exploited by anyone who passes by it.

Capilano Suspension Bridge

The suspended tracks that allow you to walk through the park on the treetops would already be enough attractions for the park. Following the logic of the enormous proportions of the Capilano Suspension Bridge Park, which takes thousands of people there every year and has these characteristics: more than 24 thousand square meters, 70 meters high and 137 meters of length. A building dating back to 1889 that has become one of the top tourist attractions in Canada.

Rocky Mountaineer Train

From North Vancouver to neighboring Whistler, it is one of the favorite itineraries of the Canadian rail trips. It takes the visitor to discover the Sea to Sky and its wonderful scenery which reaches the sky point and offers outdoor activities. The trip is comfortable and luxurious by the impeccable service and breathtaking scenery.
