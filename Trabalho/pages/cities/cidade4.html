Ottawa

Parliament Buildings

Parliament Hill is home to the Gothic-style stone buildings that make up the Canadian Parliament. The main building is a clock tower, known as Peace Tower, which is one of Ottawa's tallest buildings.

Ottawa is the capital of Canada, a dynamic city of over one million people. Located in Ontario, on the border with Quebec, it is a place where you listen to English and French languages on the streets.

The banks of the Ottawa River have a group of Gothic style buildings of extreme cultural importance and among them, in the center of the hill, the Parliament building, which attracts about 3 million visitors per year and represents the democratic system of the country, is.

There is much of the political history in the works, sculptures and ceremonies that this place harbors.

Ottawa is an organization that offers an excellent quality of life to its inhabitants. Ottawa is considered the cleanest city and one of the best cities to live in.

There are many interesting places to visit in Ottawa, for example the Canadian War Museum and the Ottawa River, which is between the city of Ottawa and Gatineau.
